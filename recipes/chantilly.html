Gorgeous Chantilly Cake

Description

Sweet chocolate moist cake frosted with one “winnah” chantilly frosting.

Ingredients

Chocolate Chiffon Cake
  • 7 eggs, separated
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup boiled water
  • 1 3/4 cup cake flour

    • ...

Chantilly Frosting
  • 2 cup evaporated milk
  • 2 cup sugar
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 cup butter

    • ...

Instructions

  1. Have all seven eggs at room temperature. Separate the egg yolk from the whites and set aside.
  2. Bring the water to a boil either by microwave or over heat. Combine it with cocoa powder and set aside to cool down.
